Whitepaper

Complete technical documentation and protocol specifications

1. Introduction

Auvex Protocol is a decentralized finance (DeFi) protocol built on smart contracts designed to provide two primary fund management mechanisms: Cycle Investment and Saving Investment, with time-based incentives, pool performance metrics, and multi-level referral networks.

The protocol is built on principles of on-chain transparency, determinism, and non-custodial operation, where all reward distribution logic, referral systems, and reward adjustments are executed directly by smart contracts without manual intervention.

This whitepaper provides detailed explanations of the protocol architecture, economic mechanisms, smart contract logic, and user interaction flows.

2. General Protocol Architecture

Auvex Protocol operates on the ERC-20 token standard (USDT) and uses a single primary smart contract that manages:

  • Recording of fund inflows and outflows
  • Management of cycle-based investments (fixed-term)
  • Management of saving investments (dynamic saving)
  • Distribution of multi-level referral rewards
  • Internal liquidity pool management
  • Reward adjustments based on pool performance

All funds managed by the protocol are reflected in two primary parameters:

  • contractBalance: Total effective funds recorded in the contract
  • poolBalance: Active funds used for paying rewards and withdrawals

3. Investment Types

3.1 Cycle Investment

Cycle Investment is an investment with fixed duration and predetermined returns. Users select one of four plans:

Plan Duration Total Return
Starter 1 Day 100.5%
Advanced 7 Days 105%
Professional 14 Days 110%
Premium 30 Days 125%

Returns are calculated using CYCLE_PERCENTS constant, with calculation basis per 1000.

Cycle Investment Flow:

  1. User makes deposit by selecting a plan
  2. Funds are recorded as a new CycleInvestment
  3. After duration ends, user can:
    • Perform claim to withdraw principal + profit
    • Perform regrow to reinvest returns into a new plan

Each Cycle Investment is independent and has a claimed status to prevent double claims.

3.2 Saving Investment

Saving Investment is a flexible investment without fixed end duration. Funds remain active until user withdraws capital.

Key characteristics:

  • Rewards paid daily
  • Reward percentage is dynamic
  • Capital can be withdrawn anytime while pool has sufficient funds

Saving Reward Percentage:

  • Minimum: 0.3% per day
  • Maximum: 0.8% per day

This percentage can increase or decrease gradually based on weekly pool performance.

4. Pool & Liquidity Mechanisms

4.1 Pool Balance

The pool serves as the primary liquidity source for:

  • Cycle reward payments
  • Saving reward payments
  • Saving capital withdrawals
  • Referral reward payments

Every net deposit adds to poolBalance. Every withdrawal reduces it.

4.2 Pool Snapshot

The protocol stores up to 30 recent snapshots of pool balance, each containing:

  • Timestamp
  • poolBalance value

These snapshots are used to calculate pool performance changes over the last 7 days.

5. Dynamic Saving Adjustment Mechanism

Each Saving Investment has a currentPercent that can change.

Adjustment Logic:

  • If pool grows 150% compared to 7 days ago percentage increases
  • If pool drops 50% compared to 7 days ago percentage decreases
  • Maximum adjustment 0.1% per week

This approach aims to maintain pool sustainability while providing performance-based incentives.

6. Referral System

Auvex Protocol implements a multi-level referral system with two separate schemes:

6.1 Cycle Referral (4 Levels)

Cycle referral rewards are calculated from Cycle Investment profits, not from principal.

Level Percentage
1 15%
2 7%
3 2%
4 1%

Qualification Requirements:

  • Referrer must be active
  • Must maintain minimum active investment according to level
  • For advanced levels, requires qualified downline count

If requirements are not met, rewards are redirected to the Scorch Referral Pool.

6.2 Saving Referral (10 Levels)

Saving referral rewards are calculated from withdrawn Saving rewards.

Level Percentage
120%

Qualification Requirements:

  • Must maintain minimum active investment according to level

If requirements are not met, rewards are redirected to the Scorch Referral Pool.

7. Scorch Referral Pool

The Scorch Referral Pool functions as a holding pool for referral rewards that don't meet qualification requirements.

Characteristics:

  • Funds are not burned
  • Remains recorded on-chain
  • Can be withdrawn by admin for protocol maintenance

This mechanism prevents unhealthy reward inflation while maintaining pool balance.

8. Regrow Mechanism

Regrow allows users to:

  • Complete a Cycle Investment that has ended
  • Reinvest total returns into a new plan

Advantages:

  • Doesn't require external transfers
  • Accelerates compounding
  • Triggers new referral distributions

Each regrow is recorded as a new Cycle Investment with separate ID.

9. User Management & Status

Each user has a data structure recording:

  • Total investment
  • Total withdrawals
  • Referral rewards
  • Total active investment
  • Active/inactive status

Active status is determined by ownership of active investments. When all capital is withdrawn, status changes to inactive.

10. Security & Protection

Auvex Protocol implements multiple protection layers:

  • ReentrancyGuard to prevent reentrancy attacks
  • Pool balance validation before payments
  • Claim status to prevent double withdrawals
  • All calculations deterministic and transparent

No hidden functions allow changes to core parameters after deployment.

11. Transparency & On-Chain Data

The protocol provides various view functions for full transparency, including:

  • User information
  • Investment details
  • Contract statistics
  • Pool and scorch pool status

All data can be verified directly through blockchain explorers.

12. Conclusion

Auvex Protocol is designed as a layered DeFi system combining:

  • Fixed return short-term cycles
  • Dynamic long-term savings
  • Qualification-based referrals
  • Pool performance-based reward adjustments

With all logic executed entirely by smart contracts, Auvex Protocol presents an ecosystem that is transparent, structured, and focused on on-chain economic sustainability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Auvex Protocol

What is Auvex Protocol?

Auvex Protocol is a DeFi platform based on smart contracts that provides structured investment mechanisms through Cycle Investment, Saving Investment, and Referral Rewards, with all processes running automatically and on-chain.

Is Auvex Protocol non-custodial?

Yes. All funds are managed directly by the smart contract, not by individuals or centralized parties. Users have full control over their investments according to contract rules.

Has the Auvex smart contract been audited?

Yes. The Auvex smart contract has undergone comprehensive manual audit and is declared PASSED Fully Correct, with no critical, high, or medium issues identified.

Does Auvex Protocol have an admin or owner?

Yes. Auvex Protocol has an admin with limited role that can only withdraw bonuses from the Scorch Referral Pool to support community sustainability. The admin does not have access to change the contract, manage user funds, or modify investment mechanisms.

Can the smart contract be upgraded or changed?

No. Auvex Protocol is immutable. There are no proxy, upgrade, or parameter change functions after contract deployment.

What token is used in Auvex Protocol?

Auvex Protocol is designed to operate using standard ERC-20 tokens, such as USDT, which follows conventional ERC-20 transfer mechanisms.

How does the Cycle Investment system work?

Users deposit into a cycle with a specific duration. After the cycle ends, users can claim principal + profit according to terms set in the smart contract.

What is Saving Investment?

Saving Investment allows users to earn daily rewards that can be claimed periodically, with flexibility to withdraw capital anytime according to contract rules.

Are rewards guaranteed to be available?

All rewards and withdrawals are limited by pool liquidity. The smart contract only allows payments if funds are available, ensuring the system remains safe and consistent.

How does the referral mechanism work in Auvex Protocol?

Referral rewards are distributed through a multi-level system with specific qualification requirements. Only referrals meeting the criteria can receive rewards.

Do referral rewards create token inflation?

No. Referral rewards do not mint new tokens. All rewards come from existing pools and are subject to contract balance checks.

Is Auvex Protocol safe from reentrancy attacks?

Yes. All sensitive functions are protected with ReentrancyGuard, making it safe from reentrancy and nested-call attacks.

Can users withdraw other people's funds?

No. Each withdrawal can only be performed by the owner of the related investment, and is verified directly by the smart contract.

Is there risk of data or reward manipulation?

No. All reward calculations, referrals, and pool operations are performed deterministically and transparently within the smart contract, without external inputs that can be manipulated.

Where can I verify data and transactions?

All Auvex Protocol activity can be verified directly through blockchain explorers, including transactions, contract balances, and smart contract function executions.

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment amount is 5 USDT for both Cycle and Saving investments.

How are referral rewards distributed?

Cycle referral rewards are calculated from profits (15%, 7%, 2%, 1% across 4 levels). Saving referral rewards are calculated from withdrawn rewards (20%, 15%, 8%, 4%, 2%, 2%, 1%, 1%, 1%, 1% across 10 levels).

What happens if referral qualification requirements are not met?

If qualification requirements are not met, referral rewards are redirected to the Scorch Referral Pool to maintain protocol sustainability.

Can I withdraw my capital from Saving Investment anytime?

Yes, you can withdraw your capital from Saving Investment anytime, provided the pool has sufficient liquidity. The investment becomes inactive after capital withdrawal.
